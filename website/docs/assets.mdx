## Usage Guidelines

### Permitted Uses

✅ **Educational presentations** - Cite AAI Wiki in slides, lectures, or teaching materials

✅ **Media coverage** - Use in articles, blog posts, or news about AAI Wiki

✅ **Social media** - Share when promoting or referencing AAI Wiki content

✅ **Academic publications** - Reference AAI Wiki in papers or posters

✅ **Conference materials** - Display in conference presentations or handouts

### Required Attribution

When using AAI Wiki assets, please include:

**For presentations:**
> "Content from AAI Wiki (aai-wiki.com)"

**For publications:**
> "AAI Wiki. [Article Title]. Available at: https://aai-wiki.com/[path]. Accessed [date]."

**For social media:**
> "@aaiwiki" or "Source: AAI Wiki"

### Restrictions

❌ **Do not** modify the logo colors or proportions

❌ **Do not** use AAI Wiki branding to imply endorsement of products or services

❌ **Do not** use as part of your own logo or branding

❌ **Do not** use in a misleading or derogatory manner
