## About This Resource

AAI Wiki is a community-driven clinical reference designed for healthcare professionals specializing in allergy, asthma, and immunology. Our content is:

- **Evidence-based**: All recommendations are grounded in current clinical guidelines and peer-reviewed research
- **Practical**: Focused on actionable information for clinical decision-making
- **Community-reviewed**: Open to contributions with expert review and discussion
- **Continuously updated**: Reflects the latest guidelines and best practices

## Contributing

We welcome contributions from healthcare professionals! See our [Contributing Guide](/contributing) to learn how to propose changes, suggest new topics, or participate in expert discussions.
