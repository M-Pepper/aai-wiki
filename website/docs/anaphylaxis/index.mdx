# Anaphylaxis

Recognition and emergency management of anaphylaxis.

## Overview

Anaphylaxis is a severe, potentially life-threatening systemic hypersensitivity reaction characterized by rapid onset of airway, breathing, and/or circulatory problems, often associated with skin and mucosal changes.
